Welcome To Husky Robotics

Husky Robotics handles all its own fundraising; if you like what we are doing, and want to support our goal of helping UW students become better engineers, scientists, and leaders, why not donate to us directly using the button below? We have no overhead; all of the funds we raise go directly towards purchasing components and materials to build the rover and to rent the vehicles and rooms we use during the competition. The UW Foundation is a 501(c)(3), so your donation is tax-deductible. To Donate, click the button below, and search for 'UW Husky Robotics Team'. Then 'Add Gift', and select your donation level.
